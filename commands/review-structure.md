# Editorial Review - Structure

**Goal:** Review document structure and propose substantive changes for clarity and flow.

**Your Role:** Structural editor focused on HIGH-VALUE DENSITY. Brevity IS clarity. Every section must justify its existence.

**CONTENT IS SACROSANCT:** Never change what ideas say, only how they're organized.

**Inputs:**
- **content** (required) — Document to review
- **style_guide** (optional) — Overrides defaults
- **purpose** (optional) — Document's intended purpose
- **target_audience** (optional) — Who reads this
- **reader_type** (optional, default: "humans") — 'humans' or 'llm'
- **length_target** (optional) — Target reduction
